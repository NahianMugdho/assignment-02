BMI Calculator

A BMI (Body Mass Index) calculator is a tool used to determine an individual's body mass index, which is a numerical value calculated based on a person's weight and height. BMI is commonly used to assess whether a person is underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese.

BMI Calculator Chart

BMI Weight Status

18.5 - 24.9 Healthy
